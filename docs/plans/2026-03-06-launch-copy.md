# RNG Chords Launch Copy Pack

## Core positioning

### Primary phrase

Random chord generator for guitar, piano, and songwriting.

### Short value prop

Roll chord progressions, hear them instantly, keep the parts you love, and export MIDI when an idea sticks.

### One-line description

RNG Chords is a playful browser-based chord generator that helps musicians find fresh progressions fast.

## Audience-specific descriptions

### Songwriters

RNG Chords helps songwriters roll a verse, chorus, or bridge idea in seconds, then keep the strongest chords and reroll the weak spots until the progression feels worth writing to.

### Guitarists

RNG Chords gives guitarists quick progression seeds, fresh voicings, and playable harmonic surprises without opening a DAW or digging through theory references first.

### Producers

RNG Chords helps producers sketch harmony fast, audition ideas immediately in the browser, and export MIDI when a progression is ready to become part of a track.

## Product Hunt draft

### Tagline

Roll random chord ideas and export the keepers.

### Short description

A playful random chord generator for guitar, piano, and songwriting.

### Full launch blurb

RNG Chords is a browser-based random chord generator for guitarists, pianists, songwriters, and producers.

You can roll a progression in seconds, hear it instantly, keep the chords that work, reroll the ones that do not, and export MIDI when an idea is worth building on.

It is designed to feel more like a musical toy than a theory exercise, so you can stumble into something good fast and keep moving.
